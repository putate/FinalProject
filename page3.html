Ice Cream Cake

Video courtesy of Tasty Youtube Channel


  • Ingredients:
  • Chocolate cookies
  • Melted chocolate
  • Vanilla ice cream

An ice cream cake is a cake made with ice cream. These can be no-bake cakes, or may be more elaborate layer cakes with ice cream between layers of sponge cake. A popular form is a three-layer cake, with a layer of ice cream between two layers of cake.

Directions
  • Crush cookies and place them at the bottom of the bowl
  • Spread the ice cream evenly throughout the bowl
  • Drizze with chocolate
  • Freeze for 4 hours
  • Now serve!!!
