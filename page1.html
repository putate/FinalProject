Peanut Butter Cups

Video courtesy of Tasty Youtube Channel



  • Ingredients:
  • Peanut butter
  • Melted chocolate
  • Powdered sugar

A classic candy confection, the peanut butter cup is one of the most popular treats in America. It was first introduced by H.B. Reese in 1928. With simple ingredients and easy preperation, anyone can make it.

Directions
  • Mix powdered sugar and peanut butter into a bowl
  • Pour melted choclate into another bowl
  • In a cupcake dish, put 1 layer of choclate, a circle of peanut butter, and then a layer of choclate on top
  • Refrigerate for 1 hour
  • Now serve!!!
